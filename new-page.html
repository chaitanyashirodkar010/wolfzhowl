Client Stated Task:

Rain Forest Foods was a superbly well intentioned brand, providing people with power foods from the Rain Forests for better & vital living. However “Power Foods” had become a fashionable fad and people were not taking it seriously. Because of its elite-class “fashionable fad” associations only, there was no emotional connect with the category and hence the band was also suffering.
